This project develops and evaluates a multimodal interactive assistance system for digital collection of patient-reported outcome measures in clinical settings.

### Focus areas

* Barrier-reduced interaction for patients
* Support for clinical integration and workflows
* Adaptation to cognitive load and communication needs
* Data quality and completion support
