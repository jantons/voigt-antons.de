This contribution examines attitudes of professional caregivers toward ICT solutions in dementia care. Survey and interview data capture perceived usefulness, ease of use, barriers to adoption, and organizational prerequisites. Results inform implementation strategies and highlight the need for training, workflow integration, and clear privacy safeguards.
