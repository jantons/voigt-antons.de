This poster outlines a tablet-supported outpatient care pathway for dementia that integrates guideline prompts, personalized monitoring, and secure communication among providers. We describe the system architecture, planned outcome measures, and stakeholder feedback from early pilots. The approach aims to enhance coordination while maintaining usability for older patients and caregivers.
