This chapter highlights approaches for developing health apps that are both user-centered and sustainable. It emphasizes the importance of involving end users in the design process to ensure usability, acceptance, and long-term relevance. By integrating user perspectives with principles of sustainable development, the work provides guidance on how digital health solutions can better align with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
