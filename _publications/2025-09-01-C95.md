This poster explores how different types of audio navigation cues in augmented reality affect users’ experience and perceived usefulness. The study evaluates spatialized sound, verbal instructions, and symbolic audio cues, analyzing their impact on navigation performance, cognitive workload, and overall user satisfaction.
